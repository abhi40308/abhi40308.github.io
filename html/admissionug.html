Admissions
IIIT Kalyani Students come from across India, representing diverse experiences, backgrounds and cultures.
The academic structure of IIIT Kalyani exhibits the diversity, comprehensiveness, and depth befitting an institution of its age and stature. The programme/course offered at the institute have the flexibility to evolve and change in response to new requirements. They serve the dual purpose of building a solid foundation of knowledge and of enhancing confidence, creativity and innovation in its students.

A unique attribute of IIIT Kalyani's academic structure is the autonomy enjoyed by the faculty in designing and structuring their curriculum and courses . All courses, however, need to be approved by the senate. A favourable student-teacher ratio ensures productive, personal contact between the student and the teacher.

Finally, credit-based academic programmes offer flexibility to students to progress at their own pace. This system ensures an academic programme which is dynamic and constantly evolving and which reflects the institute’s commitment to stay in tune with the expanding frontiers of knowledge worldwide. The institute provides high quality of education, and it ensures that its students are also of exceptionally high calibre. Admission to IIIT Kalyani is enabled through Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) only for the undergraduate programme.

IIIT Kalyani's B.Tech. programme admits 130 students for only one of its discipline, Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). Seats are filled up through Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) after qualifying JEE (Main) examination administered by Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

Please bring the following documents as outlined in JoSAA website for reporting and admission :-
( If any of the documents are found invalid or wrong, the allotted seat is liable to be cancelled )


1. Three passport size photographs identical to the one pasted on the JEE (Main) 2017 application form.

2. Class XII performance check [Annexure 7(b) of JoSAA Business rules]

3. Provisional Seat Allotment Letter

4. Undertaking by the candidate [Annexure 9 of JoSAA Business rules]

5. (i) Proof of initial fee (Rs. 45,000 for General, Rs. 20,000 for SC/ST/PWD) payment (ii) Proof of Special round participation fee (applicable for special round only)

6. Original documents for verification (submit self-attested photocopies):
i)Aadhaar Card
ii)Admit Card of JEE (Main)-2017
iii)Score Card of JEE (Main)-2017 issued by CBSE (downloaded score card is acceptable)
iv) Class X (High School) Board Certificate
v) Class XII or equivalent examination Certificate and Marks sheet
vi) Certificate of category of SC/ST (Central list), if applicable, issued by the competent authority in the prescribed format (as given in josaa.nic.in)
vii) Certificate of category of OBC (Central list), if applicable, issued by the competent authority in the prescribed format (as given in josaa.nic.in). The certificate should clearly mention that the candidate belongs to Non Creamy Layer (NCL). The certificate should have been issued on or after 1st April, 2017. Non Creamy Layer OBC Certificate issued prior to 1st April 2017 shall not be accepted in any case.
viii) Certificate for person with Disabilities (Pwd), if applicable. The Certificate should be issued by a Registered Medical Practitioner. The Certificate will be rejected if the disability is less than 40%.

7. Medical Certificate (Original, Annexure 8 of JoSAA Business Rules)

8. Printout of locked choices and terms and conditions duly signed by the candidate (applicable to those getting seat allotments for the first time in seventh or special round)
Download admission form for admission to B.Tech. program , after you have been allotted a seat by CSAB in final round.
Students can fill in the details, take a printout, sign (student and parents) and submit it at the time of admission.

Financial Aid
The Institute provides information to its students for obtaining scholarships under the scheme of Central and State Governments.
Since State Government and Central Government agencies have a large number of scheme to provide scholarship to students, the admission office will help the students to apply for such opportunities.
    Some of the information on financial aids can be found below:
  • Vidya Lakshmi Portal.
  • Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojna:
    • The eligibility criteria for the students are:
    Student should be domicile of Madhya Pradesh .
    Student must secure 75% or more(in case of M.P. Board) and 85% or more (in case of CBSE/ICSE) .
    Total income should be less than 6 Lacs.
    The details of the scheme can be found here.
