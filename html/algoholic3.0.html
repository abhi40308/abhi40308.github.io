About this club

Algoholic is IIIT Kalyani's coding club.The aim of Algoholic is to promote programming as a culture in the Institute. The club brings people together, so that they may sharpen their skills in programming. This is the performance sheet achieved by IIIT Kalyani teams in ACM ICPC Online Preliminary Round 2016 which is quite simply the worldâ€™s oldest, largest, and most prestigious programming contest.

What we do?

We conduct sessions and workshops, hold lectures and talks, and even host coding competitions and hackathons. There is something for everyone. Whether you want to learn, participate in hackathons and win big prizes, or prepare for the ACM-ICPC, you can do it all here. We have a list of events planned up for the coming year. Keep watching this space to know more.
