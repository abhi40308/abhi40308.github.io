SOFTWARE AND HARDWARE LABS

Knowledge is power but without implementation it is futile. IIIT Kalyani promotes learning in all ways. The institute is equipped with software and hardware labs. There are three software and three hardware labs.

Software Lab

As the institute has Computer Science and Engineering stream presently its software labs are of a great importance for the institution. The software labs are facilitated with educational software and are powered by Windows and Linux.The linux platform improves the scope of learning. The labs are equipped with projectors for video conferencing and workshops.

Electronic Lab

One of the core subject for computer science and engineering is electronics. The hardware labs are well equipped with instruments used for electronics, digital systems and embedded systems experiments. Soon the hardware for computer architechture will be available in the campus.
