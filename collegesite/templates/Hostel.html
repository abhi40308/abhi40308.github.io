HOSTEL

The institute provides accommodation for outstation students on payment basis in rented apartments. Separate accommodation is provided for boys and girls students.The institute has hired four hostels, three for boys and one for girls. The hostels are well situated accessible to all conviniences (playgrounds, market, railway station, bus stops)
