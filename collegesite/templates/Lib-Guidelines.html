GENERAL LIBRARY RULES

  1. Library Member should leave their personal belongings; bags, briefcases, personal books and parcel near the counter reserved for this purpose before entering into the library. They may keep loose papers, notebooks and Laptop (without bag).
  2. Mobile phone should be switched off in the library. Use of mobile phone in the library is strictly prohibited.
  3. Books or other materials taken from the stacks should not be re-shelved by the readers. Books should be left on the reading table after use.
  4. Readers should maintain peace in the library and should not disturb others in any way.
  5. Readers are requested to follow the Queue System at Circulation Counter.
  6. Readers should not deface, mark, cut, mutilate or damage library material in any way. If anyone is found doing so, he/she will be charged the full replacement cost of the material.
  7. Smoking and eatables are strictly prohibited.
  8. Chairs and tables should not be disturbed from their positions.
  9. Readers are requested to sit in a proper way while in the library. They should avoid resting their feet on tables, chairs, shelves, window sills
  10. Do not move books/journals from its specific area to another area.
  11. Readers are requested to return the books on the time before the due date stipulated over the due date slip pasted on the last page of the book.
  12. Keep the Library premises tidy.
