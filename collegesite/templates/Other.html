NETWORK FACILITY

The institute is facilitated with a broadband connection and WiFi connectivity all around the campus.Students have thier individual login id for accessing the WiFi. The Labs are also connected to a LAN connection which provides a fast internet access to the Desktops

SPORTS

The Institution encourages sports in all ways. The college has its own equipments for sports. The Institution induces the spirit of sports in the students by encouraging them to participate in the inter college sports competitions like the Inter IIIT sports meet and GUSTO organised by IIIT Jabalpur. The town has a stadium and number of grounds for the students for the sports activities.As the campus is still under constrution the students are encouraged to play in these grounds.Regular events occur at these grounds between various colleges. In Kalyani there are all sorts of arrangement for the encouragment of students in sports.

TRANSPORT FACILITY

The institute provides the facility of transport from hostel to the campus. The timings of the bus are flexible with the time table. The institute also provide the transport facility for attending seminars or events nearby.

LOCATIONAL ADVANTAGES

IIIT Kalyani has been set up at Kalyani, which is at a valid distance from the Kolkata. Kalyani is been emerging as a educational hub in West Bengal. National Institutions like AIIMS, IISER , government institutions like JNM, University of Kalyani and private institutions like JIS college of engineering have been established here Kalyani is a well planned and developed town. It has all facilities like the malls, restaurants, parks, gyms, swimming pool , grounds, stadium and a good transportation for the better connectivity.

CAFETERIA

The building has a canteen in it. Cafeteria provides different dishes and drinks.

GUEST HOUSE

The institute has its own guest house to provide accomodation for guest faculty, deligates who come for seminar, workshop organisers and parents.
